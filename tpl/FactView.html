Pothole Fact:

A pothole is a shallow pit or depression produced in a roadway surface by the effects of wear-and-tear and weather.

Potholes are a major factor in causing axle & suspension failure, which counts for a third of mechanical issues on the roads.

Traffic Light Fact:

Car crashes from red light runners are more likely than any other type of crash (47 percent versus 33 percent) to produce some degree of injury.

The first traffic lights were installed in front of the British House of Parliament in 1868. Engineer John Peake Knight designed the traffic light to regulate horse-drawn carriage traffic.

Bridge Fact:

Bridge designs depend on their intended function, financial resources and also the type of terrain where they are constructed.

The Zhaozhou Bridge is the oldest standing bridge in China and the world's oldest stone segmental arch bridge. Built in 605 AD, it is still standing strong today, over 1400 years later.
